Marketing and Arts Management Intern with Zenon Dance Company and School

As a marketing and arts management intern, I helped with social media marketing. This required regularly utilizing Adobe Photoshop to create post templates, update company member and teacher bios, and take photographs and videos for promotional uses. This video was a main project from my time working with the company. It is a montage of previous footage in celebration of the company's 35th Anniversary. This time capsule was posted to all social sites, with a shorter version on Instagram, where it performed extremely well.
