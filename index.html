A personal brand of digital works

Moving since the age of three, I have always had a creative streak. As a dancer, the process of collaboration and creation is readily familiar as it is such an integral part of performance and choreographing. The design process is similar to that of the choreographic process; drafting ideas, pitching and presenting incomplete drafts, receiving feedback, and working with others to make the best possible, cohesive outcome. Recently, digital design, front-end web development, marketing, and branding have been the channels for my creativity.

What is SEND?

SEND, Sabrina Easley Novel Designs, is my own personal brand. It encompasses all of my digital and creative endeavors, and this portfolio of my works seeks to curate towards this brand identity. I strive to offer branding, marketing, creative, and digital services with vigor. Seeking any of these services? You came to the right place.

Social Media Coordinator for Her Campus St. Olaf

Her Campus is a nation-wide publication catering towards collegiette women, and there are multiple branches for several colleges and universities. The branch at St. Olaf publishes pieces from current events to DIY projects to meditation methods for de-stressing. My role with this branch of the publication is running the Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram accounts as well as writing the occassional sponsored article.

New Media/Editorial Intern with Psychopomp Literary Magazine

From July of 2017 up through January of 2018, I helped the staff at Psychopomp by copyediting submissions, writing reviews on contemporary novels, and creating graphics to be utilized in posts and reviews. I also created and posted to the magazine's Instagram account.

Marketing and Arts Management Intern with Zenon Dance Company and School

As a marketing and arts management intern, I helped with social media marketing. This time capsule was posted to all social sites, with a shorter version on Instagram, where it performed extremely well.
