New Media/Editorial Intern with Psychopomp Literary Magazine

From July of 2017 up through January of 2018, I helped the staff at Psychopomp by copyediting submissions, writing reviews on contemporary novels, and creating graphics to be utilized in posts and reviews. I also created and posted to the magazine's Instagram account.

Literary Reviews:
Anne Valente's Our Hearts Will Burn Us Down
